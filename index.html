01 // IDENTITY

The Bridge Between Stability and Crisis.

Founded by Tyson Mandela Murray, HumanitAI CIC (SIC 88.99) exists to improve the physical, mental, social, and digital wellbeing of residents in Bath & North East Somerset.

We do not merely run sessions. We create human infrastructure that helps vulnerable adults regain functionality, confidence, and healthier participation in society.
